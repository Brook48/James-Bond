James Bond

Summary

James Bond (007) is a sophisticated, resourceful British MI6 secret agent, created by Ian Fleming, known for his "license to kill," high-tech gadgets (from Q), cool cars, exotic missions, and femme fatales, who thwarts global villains, often involving nuclear threats or world domination, evolving from a Cold War spy to a modern, complex hero in numerous books and films.
